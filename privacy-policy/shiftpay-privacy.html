ShiftPay — Privacy Policy Effective date: 17 January 2026 Developer: VitaSync Labs Contact: vitasynclabs@gmail.com 1. Information We Collect a. Account Information (Optional) If you choose to use Backup & Sync, you may sign in with your Google account. When you do this, we process: Your Google account email address A Firebase user ID associated with your account This information is used solely to authenticate your account and enable cross-device synchronisation. b. User-Generated Content When you use ShiftPay, you may enter: Shift times and patterns Overtime entries Leave and holiday records Pay rates and multipliers Contracted hours Optional notes If Backup & Sync (Pro Beta) is enabled, this information is securely stored in Google Firebase to allow syncing across your devices. If Backup & Sync is not enabled, this data remains stored locally on your device. c. Device & Technical Information We use Google Firebase services which may collect limited technical data, including: App instance identifiers Device identifiers Device model and operating system version Crash reports (Firebase Crashlytics) Diagnostic data Optional analytics events (Firebase Analytics, if enabled) This information does not include your shift or pay records. d. Subscription Information Subscriptions are processed securely through Google Play Billing. We do not receive or store your payment card details. 2. How We Use Your Data We use data to: Provide shift and overtime calculations Enable Backup & Sync across devices Maintain your account Improve stability and performance Diagnose technical issues We do not use your data for advertising or profiling. 3. Data Storage Local data is stored securely on your device. Synced data (if enabled) is stored in Google Firebase. All data transmitted to Firebase is encrypted in transit using HTTPS/TLS. 4. Data Sharing We do not sell or trade your data. Data may be processed by: Google Firebase (authentication, database, analytics, crash reporting) Google Play (subscription processing) These providers act as service processors. No advertisers, employers, or third parties have access to your personal shift data. 5. Account & Data Deletion If you use Google Sign-In, you may request deletion of your account and associated cloud data. To request deletion, visit: https://shiftpay.web.app/delete-account You may also remove local data at any time by uninstalling the app. 6. Retention Local data: retained until deleted or the app is uninstalled. Synced cloud data: retained until account deletion or data removal. Crash and analytics data: retained under Firebase’s standard retention policies. 7. Children’s Privacy ShiftPay is not intended for children under 13 (or the minimum age required in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect data from children. 8. Legal Rights If you are located in the UK, EEA, or similar jurisdictions, you may have rights under data protection laws, including access, correction, and deletion. California residents may have rights under CCPA/CPRA where applicable. 9. Security We implement reasonable safeguards to protect your information.
