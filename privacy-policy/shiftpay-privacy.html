ShiftPay — Privacy Policy

Effective date: 17 January 2026 • Developer: VitaSync Labs — vitasynclabs@gmail.com

1. Information We Collect

a. User-Provided Data

When you use ShiftPay, you may choose to enter information such as:

  • Shift times and patterns
  • Overtime and holiday entries
  • Pay rates, premiums, and multipliers
  • Contracted hours and schedules
  • Optional notes and work history details

b. Shared Holiday Data (Optional Feature)

If you enable the shared holidays feature, limited holiday-related information is uploaded to Google Firebase so it can be shared across devices or with selected users.

This data may include:

  • Holiday dates
  • Holiday type (for example: leave or time off)
  • A non-identifying shared reference ID

No pay data, shift details, notes, or personal identifiers are shared. Shared holiday data is uploaded only when you actively use this feature.

c. Device-Generated Data

ShiftPay uses Google Firebase services which may collect limited technical data, including:

  • Crash logs (Firebase Crashlytics)
  • App instance identifiers
  • Device and operating system information
  • Optional, anonymised analytics events (Firebase Analytics, if enabled)

This data does not include your personal shift, pay, or holiday records.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • Calculate and display shifts, overtime, holidays, and totals
  • Synchronise shared holiday entries when the feature is enabled
  • Display summaries, history, and reports in the app
  • Improve app stability and performance
  • Diagnose and fix technical problems

3. Data Storage

Shift, overtime, and pay data is stored locally on your device. Shared holiday data is stored securely in Firebase only when the feature is enabled. VitaSync Labs does not access your personal pay or shift history.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. Data may be shared only with:

  • Google Firebase — for shared holiday synchronisation, crash diagnostics, and optional analytics

No employers, advertisers, or other third parties have access to your data.

5. Holidays & Overtime

Overtime entries are always stored locally on your device. Holiday entries are stored locally unless you enable shared holidays. All calculations and reports are performed on-device.

6. Children’s Privacy

ShiftPay is not intended for children under 13, or under the minimum age required in your country. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

7. Legal Bases & Your Rights

If you are located in the UK, EEA, or similar jurisdictions:

  • Shared holiday processing is based on user action and consent
  • Crash diagnostics are processed under legitimate interests
  • Analytics are collected only where consent is required and provided

California residents may exercise rights under the CCPA where applicable.

8. Retention

  • On-device data: kept until you delete it or uninstall the app
  • Shared holiday data: retained until you remove it or disable the feature
  • Crash and analytics data: retained by Firebase under their standard policies

9. Security

On-device data remains within the app sandbox. Communication with Firebase uses HTTPS/TLS encryption. Access to shared holiday data is restricted by Firebase security rules.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Updates will be published on this page and may be noted in app release notes.

11. Contact
