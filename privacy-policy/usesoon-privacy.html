UseSoon — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 24 April 2026

This privacy policy applies to the UseSoon Android app ("UseSoon", "the app", "we").

1. Who we are

UseSoon is published by Vitasync Labs. You can contact us at vitasynclabs@gmail.com.

2. Accounts

UseSoon does not require an account. There is no sign-up, no sign-in, no user profile, and no password.

3. What information the app handles

3.1 Food item data (stored locally on your device)

When you add an item — by scanning a barcode or entering it manually — the app stores the following on your device only:

  • the product name you choose
  • the expiry date you choose
  • the storage location you choose (fridge, freezer, cupboard)
  • the category you choose
  • optionally, the barcode number for that item
  • optionally, a product image URL
  • timestamps for when you created, updated, used, or were reminded about the item

This data is kept in a local database on your device. It is not sent to our servers. We do not operate servers for UseSoon.

3.2 Barcode lookups (sent to Open Food Facts)

When you scan a barcode, the app sends the barcode number to the Open Food Facts API at world.openfoodfacts.org to try to identify the product. Open Food Facts is a public, open-source food database operated by a non-profit.

  • We do not send any personal information with the barcode.
  • We do not send your device identifiers, account, email, or location.
  • We identify our app to Open Food Facts with a User-Agent string that includes our contact email (vitasynclabs@gmail.com), as Open Food Facts requests of API clients.
  • The barcode result we receive (product name, brand, image URL, category) is cached on your device so we do not re-query the same barcode unnecessarily.

You can avoid barcode lookups entirely by only adding items manually.

Open Food Facts has its own privacy policy: https://world.openfoodfacts.org/privacy

3.3 Product images

If Open Food Facts returns an image URL for a scanned product, the app loads that image directly from the Open Food Facts image server on demand. No image is uploaded by you.

3.4 Notifications

Reminders are local notifications scheduled and delivered by your device. The app reads its own local database at the reminder time and generates the notification on-device. No reminder content is sent over the internet.

3.5 Crash and analytics data

UseSoon does not include any analytics SDK, advertising SDK, or crash reporter. We do not collect usage data.

4. What we do not do

  • We do not operate accounts or cloud sync.
  • We do not show ads.
  • We do not share or sell your data.
  • We do not track you across apps or websites.
  • We do not use third-party advertising networks.
  • We do not collect your contacts, location, photos, microphone, or device identifiers.

5. Permissions the app requests

  • Internet — to look up barcodes via Open Food Facts and load product images.
  • Post notifications (Android 13 and later) — to show the daily reminder digest you opted into. You can revoke this in system settings at any time.

The app uses the Google Code Scanner for barcode scanning. Google Code Scanner handles the camera in a separate system component, which is why UseSoon does not ask for camera permission.

6. Children

UseSoon is a household food-management tool. It is not directed at children under 13 and we do not knowingly collect data from children.

7. Data retention and deletion

All food item data lives on your device.

  • Delete an item — tap the item and choose Delete.
  • Delete all app data — uninstall UseSoon, or use Android's system settings (Settings → Apps → UseSoon → Storage → Clear storage).

Because we do not operate servers, there is no server-side data to delete. Uninstalling the app removes everything UseSoon stores about you from your device.

If Google Drive backup is enabled on your device, a backup copy of the app's local data may exist in your own Google Drive account under your control. You can remove it at https://drive.google.com/drive/backups.

8. Security in transit

Barcode lookups and product-image fetches to Open Food Facts use HTTPS.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy to reflect changes in the app. Material changes will be communicated in-app or via the app's Play Store listing.

10. Contact

Questions, requests, or concerns: vitasynclabs@gmail.com.
