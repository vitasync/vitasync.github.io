CarbSync: Food Log — Privacy Policy

Effective date: 30 August 2025 • Developer: VitaSync Labs — vitasynclabs@gmail.com

1. Information We Collect

a. User-Provided Data

Profile information (e.g., age, sex, country, dietary preferences), meal/supplement logs, settings.

b. Device-Generated Data

Crash reports (Firebase Crashlytics), anonymous usage stats (Firebase Analytics). Camera input for barcodes (images not stored by us).

2. How We Use Your Data

Provide logging features and insights, and improve app reliability.

3. Data Storage

Your logs/profile stay on your device. Analytics/crash data are stored by Google Firebase.

4. Barcode & AI Services

Barcodes/queries may be sent to public food databases or AI services; no personally identifiable data is shared.

5. Data Sharing

No selling/renting personal data. Limited technical data may be shared with Firebase and ML providers to deliver features.

6. Children’s Privacy

Not intended for children under 13.

7. GDPR

EU users may access/correct/delete data: vitasynclabs@gmail.com.

8. Changes

We may update this policy and note changes in the app or release notes.

9. Contact

vitasynclabs@gmail.com
